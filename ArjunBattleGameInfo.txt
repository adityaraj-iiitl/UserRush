# 🎮 Mahabharat Archery Game

A fast-paced browser-based game inspired by the Mahabharat, where the player controls **Arjuna** and defends against waves of enemies using precise timing and strategy.

---

## 🚀 Live Demo

👉 https://user-rusharjunbattle.vercel.app/

---

## 🧠 About the Project

This game focuses on creating an engaging and replayable experience by combining:

* Smooth gameplay mechanics
* Increasing difficulty over time
* Character-inspired power-ups

The player must survive as long as possible while maximizing score and reacting quickly to incoming enemies.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🏹 **Dynamic Gameplay** – Continuous enemy spawning with increasing difficulty
* 💥 **Combat System** – Arrow shooting with collision detection
* 📈 **Score System** – Real-time score updates
* 🏆 **High Score Tracking** – Stored using localStorage
* ⚡ **Power-ups** – Special support characters like Krishna and Karna
* 🎮 **Replayability** – Designed for multiple playthroughs
* 📱 **Responsive UI** – Works across devices
